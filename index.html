Utility

Open links via about:blank.

Type a URL and click Open in about:blank. Your browser will open a new blank tab/window and then navigate it to the target address.

Tip: include https://. Some sites block being opened in certain contexts.

Status: Ready.

Notes & limits

  • This only works reliably when triggered by a direct user action (click/tap).
  • Pop-up blockers may prevent window.open. If blocked, use “Open here instead”.
  • Some URLs may refuse to load or behave differently due to site security policies.
  • We don’t embed external pages in an iframe here—this just navigates a new blank tab.
